National Dashboard

The Ethiopian National Dashboard is a comprehensive and dynamic platform designed to provide the nation's key development outcomes. It is developed with the aim of enhancing data access for better decision-making, and supporting development tracking. This dashboard aggregates data from various sectors including macro, economic, infrastructure and projects, social sectors, and governance and civil service, environment, and climate.
